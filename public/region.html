East of England

East of England is a region.

Council areas in this region are: Thurrock, Southend-on-Sea, Essex, Hertfordshire, Luton, Bedford, Central Bedfordshire, Cambridgeshire, Peterborough, Norfolk and Suffolk.

Population: 5,847,000
Ethnic minorities: 5.5%

Compared to the average, people of an ethnic minority in Basildon were:

  • less likely to be employed
  • more likely to be in receipt of state support
  • more likely to be stopped and searched
