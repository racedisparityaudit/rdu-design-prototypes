Basildon

According to the 2011 Census, 93% of residents in Basildon are White, and 7% belong to Black, Asian, Mixed or Other ethnic group.

 

Work, pay and benefits

The main facts and figures show that:

  • in 2016, a larger percentage of White British and White ethnic minority groups were employed than the other ethnic groups combined
  • in 2016, White people in Basildon were less likely to be unemployed than those from other ethnic groups

Housing

The main facts and figures show that:

  • 15% of homeless households in Basildon were from an Asian, Black, Mixed or Other ethnicity in 2016/17, and 85% were from a White ethnicity
  • The percentage of homeless households in Basildon from a non-White ethnicity has been increasingly over the past 5 years
  • 91% of new social housing went to White households
