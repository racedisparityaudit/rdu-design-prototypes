About ethnicity

The population of England and Wales is made up of different ethnic groups, or ethnicities.

This overview covers how ethnicity is defined, how data about ethnicity is gathered, and how this has changed over time.

The section also gives an overall picture of the population by ethnicity.It analyses the different ethnic groups by age and sex and answers questions about income, birthplace, home and family.
